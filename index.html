Word Shuffle

Guess the word!

Choose a level: easy(4-5 letters), medium(6-7 letters) or hard(8-9 letters).
Each difficulty is tied to a specific category, and contains 15 words.
A word will be chosen randomly, where the letters will be shuffled around.
Your task is to guess what word it is and score as many points as possible. Guess all the words right and you've won the game.
You will have 5 lives in total. Guess wrong and lose a life, reach 0 lives and the game will be over!
